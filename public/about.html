About Me

Hi There. I'm Dirk Oliver de Klerk, but you can simply call me Dirk. Allow me to share a bit about myself and what drives me.

Background

I hail from the picturesque town of Vredendal in the Western Cape province of South Africa, where I was raised with Afrikaans as my mother tongue. My family background is diverse; my mother is a dedicated home education teacher at a local high school, while my father is a sheep farmer in the Northern Cape province. This upbringing instilled in me a strong work ethic and a deep connection to my roots.

Eductation and Profession

I pursued my passion for problem-solving and technology by studying Industrial Engineering at Stellenbosch University. Today, I work as an IT Security professional specializing in Privilege Access Management (PAM). I'm genuinely passionate about this field and the opportunity it offers to make the digital world a safer place.

Aspiring Fullstack Developer

Beyond my IT security work, I'm on an exciting journey as an aspiring Fullstack developer. This website serves as the canvas for my development work, where I challenge the status quo and aim to create innovative solutions.

Motto

I live by the motto of challenging the status quo. Combining my love for IT security and development allows me to shed light on unconventional perspectives and contribute to making the world a better place to live in.
