Dirk's Best Movies

These are my top 3 movies of all time

The Good Year

This one stars Russel Growe and is based in France. Such a goodie and is the main reason why France is on my travel bucket list.

Spirit

This is my favorite animation film ever. You try and watch it without crying! Love the Bryan Adams soundtrack.

The Shawshank Redemption

This 1994 masterpiece starring Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman is a must. A tale of hope and friendship set in a prison.
